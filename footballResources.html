About Our Program

Our village football program is dedicated to providing a fun, safe, and encouraging environment for children to learn and enjoy the beautiful game. We focus on developing not just football skills, but also important values like teamwork, discipline, and sportsmanship. Every child is welcome, regardless of their skill level.

Improve Your Skills: Soccer Drills

Soccer Drill Playlist

With two partners, form a triangle. Pass the ball to one partner, receive it back, and then pass to the other. Keep the passes sharp and on the ground.

Zinc Football Academy Selections

Organization: Zinc Football Academy
Level: Elite Residential Academy (I-League 3)
Based in Zawar, this is one of India's top-rated academies. They are the Rajasthan state champions. They recruit talented young players for their fully residential program. Keep an eye on their news for scouting events.

State Level Championships

Organization: Rajasthan Football Association (RFA)
Level: Official State Competitions
The RFA organizes official state leagues and championships for different age groups (Sub-Junior, Junior, Senior). Winning here is how teams qualify for national competitions. All serious players should follow the RFA website for announcements.
